    Class Types

    >

    Private | 1 Student

    6 months – 3 years : 20 Minutes

    3+ years : 30 Minutes

    An individual class for your kid’s needs and goals. This class can be fast paced or relaxed,
    with skills and exercises tailored specifically to developing your little swimmer’s capabilities.

     

    Semi-Private | 2 Students

    30 Minutes

    Lessons for two please! With this shared 30 minute class, your child gets to learn water safety
    and swim technique with a little company. This type of lesson works best with students who are
    one or two skill levels apart.

     

    Group | 3-5 Students

    30 Minutes

    By creating healthy competition, your swimmer is motivated by watching their peers
    and trying to emulate the skills being worked on. This is great for the visual learners.
    Students attend the same location, session dates, and class time.

    Group classes must be put together by the client, who provides the students needed to create the class.
    *Did you put together the group? Ask about the “Organizer Reward”
