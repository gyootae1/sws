    Policies

    Cancellation Due To Weather:

     

    Classes will not be cancelled due to inclement weather UNTIL 1 hour before your scheduled class.

    Decision to cancel will be based on the precipitation chances of 65% or higher* for the hour of your class time.

    *AccuWeather.com will be used to determine weather conditions

     

    Class Cancellations/Make – Ups:

     

    The instructor must be the one to cancel your lesson to receive a FREE make up class.

    Classes that are cancelled for any reason, by the parent, after the start of a session, can be made up for for a fee. * See price list

     

    Session Cancellations:

     

    You may cancel your entire session for a full refund up until 48 business hours prior to the first class of your session.

    Classes are non-refundable after the start date of the session.
