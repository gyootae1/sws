    Meet Miss Shea!

    “My classes are paced toward each child’s readiness and will expose students to a variety of skills tailored specifically to their needs, goals, or preferences.” -Shea

    Shea’s experience in aquatics began when she was 4 years old, as a student herself. Her professional work in aquatics began in 2007, after training for her first swim instructor certification and earning her CPR certification. Since then Shea has worked with infants, toddlers, children, and yep, even parents, developing their swim skills at all levels. She has also found recognition with the United States Swim School Association by earning certifications in Infant and Toddler Aquatic Theory as an Instructor/Trainer.
